About Me

Hi! I'm Micah Whiting. I'm a 3D Modeler from the Rochester New York area. My area of expertise includes asset modeling, sculpting, UV mapping, texturing, world building, and environment modeling. To do this I prefer to use 3DS Max, Mudbox, Photoshop, and Unreal Engine 4. However, I am also open to using other programs, software, and techniques.
