Products

At Acadia Park Equipment Co., we provide a line of premium products at prices that fit anyone’s budget. Using only high-grade materials in all of our products, we provide outdoor wear that is first in class for quality while bypassing the hefty price tag of other brands.

Bring everything you need for an action-packed day in any of our backpacks. Our packs are constructed of hyper-durable nylon and crafted with a haul handle on the exterior for quick grab-and-go. Features include fleece-lined front organizational compartments, a larger main compartment, and a dedicated mobile-office for your laptop, tablet and documents.

Designed to maximize your mobility, Acadia’s line of specially crafted outdoor pants are made with durable yarns, feature articulated knees for freedom of movement, and are completely impervious to rain, sleet and snow, while allowing perspiration to pass through from within. Our design is durable, moisture-wicking, breathable, and wrinkle free.

Acadia’s brand of jackets keeps you warm on your coldest and wettest adventure. With high loftiness, moisture resistance, and the ability to regain loft after compression, the fill power of our specially packed goose down provides excellent warmth-to-weight ratio and compressibility, both critical to retain warmth and furnish comfort.

Take an Acadia tent as your base of operations on your next outdoor getaway! Our durable line of tents ranges from personal, single sleeper enclosures, to tents with room for six. With high ceilings, large windows for expansive views, exceptional airflow, and a design that packs into a duffel-bag-style stuffsack for easy transport, our tents are perfect for any adventure you have planned.
