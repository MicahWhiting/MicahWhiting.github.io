Mug UV Layout

A photorealistic model of a ceramic mug with multiple PBR textures. The model was created in Blender and comes with multiple PBR textures that were created in Substance Painter and Photoshop. The goal of this project was to create a model with multiple interchangeable textures without overlapping UVs. I did this by following the industry standard process of building and texturing the mug from real world references while also conducting research throughout the process to ensure that I utilized the correct dimensions. This model and its multiple textures can be purchased here.

Maps: Diffuse, Metalness, Roughness. Height, Normal.

Polys: 9,484

Vertices: 9,467
