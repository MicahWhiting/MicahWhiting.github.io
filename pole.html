Wooden Electric Pole Concept Art

A photorealistic model of a wooden electric pole found in outdoor settings next to roadways. The model was created in 3DS Max and textured in Mudbox. The goal of this project was to demonstrate an ability to create assets for a virtual environment while following the industry standard process of building and texturing props from references. Research was conducted throughout this process to aid in maintaining a high level of detail and for the creation of textures. The final result was a detailed model that can be utilized across an environment.

LODs: 3

Maps: Diffuse, Normal, Specular, Gloss.

Polys: 4,001

Vertices: 3,923
